## 📝 Document Summaries

### Certificate of Incorporation
- **Entity:** BlackRoad OS, Inc.
- **State:** Delaware
- **Type:** C-Corporation
- **Purpose:** General business purposes
- **Stock:** Authorized shares structure defined

### Section 83(b) Election ⚡ CRITICAL
- **Purpose:** Elect to pay taxes on stock NOW (at formation) instead of at vesting
- **Benefit:** Massive tax savings if company grows
- **Deadline:** Must file within 30 days of stock grant
- **Status:** ✅ FILED (Keep proof forever!)

### CIIAA (Confidential Information and Invention Assignment)
- **Purpose:** Ensures all IP created belongs to company
- **Coverage:** Inventions, code, ideas, business plans
- **Critical for:** Fundraising, M&A, investor confidence

### Indemnification Agreement
- **Purpose:** Protects directors/officers from personal liability
- **Coverage:** Legal expenses, settlements, judgments
- **Standard:** Delaware law maximum protection
